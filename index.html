Serverside@engineering:~$

Serverside Engineering Community is focused on the superheroes who build applications on the backend performing the necessary magic to ensure the APIs return the right content. We fight brutal wars to keep the servers up and protect the territorial integrity of our application while safeguarding the data and information of our users. We do everything it takes to keep the web safe.

The idea to have serverside conversations came from the need to have more structures around conversations we have had on twitter over the years, and to also share ideas and approaches to addresses different problems we have encountered building applications for our community. Our goal is to learn from each other and get better through shared experiences. After all, we as a community are only as strong as the weakest of us.

We are having the first serverisde conversation in February and it is an invite only event. The conversations are also only targeted at Intermediate and Senior Backend Engineers.
